About Us

Raising the standard of cleaning and pest management in Kenya

Your Trusted Partner in Hygiene and Protection

Adalbrad Clean Agency Limited is a full-service Pest Control, Cleaning and Disinfection company committed to delivering superior hygiene and protection to institutions, farms, public spaces, homes, and home-apartments.

We combine globally accredited, nationally certified products and modern technologies with highly trained personnel to provide services that are efficient, safe, and of consistently high quality.

Founded with the vision of raising the standard of cleaning and pest management in Kenya, we believe clean environments are not simply aesthetic—they are essential to health, productivity, safety, and peace of mind.
